Our mission

In creating a base on mars, we will be unlocking a new world of exploration into space. Not only does it open up new scientific and economic avenues, but it also will help bring mankind together in a singular effort.

A mars base opens up multiple important economic avenues:

  • Space Tourism
  • Energy Production
  • Resource Extraction

More importantly, it will also open the door to unlocking future space exploration:

  • assess the health impacts of living in space
  • learn how to build and operate a base on another celestial body
  • test site for new technologies and spacecraft
